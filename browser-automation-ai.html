Orbit AI Browser Automation Tool

Orbit AI is a browser automation AI workspace for page-aware research, summaries, agent workflows, repetitive task support, and productivity automation.

Browser Automation with AI Context

Orbit helps users work across websites, documents, research pages, and workflow steps with AI assistance. The goal is to reduce manual browser switching and help users turn page context into answers, plans, summaries, drafts, and next actions.

Where Orbit Fits

  • Page-aware research and summarization
  • AI agents for multi-step browser workflows
  • Workflow planning and repetitive task support
  • File analysis and browser companion workflows
  • Desktop and web workspace access

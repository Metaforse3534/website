// our story

Building the future of AI, one release at a time.

Orbit began with one idea: AI should do more than answer questions. It should help people build, automate, create, and get real work done from one intelligent workspace.

2026
150,000+ users
Orbit reached more than 150,000 users worldwide. With AI agents, browser automation, desktop control, and an expanding ecosystem, Orbit became a complete AI workspace for developers, creators, students, and businesses.
Early 2025
The first prototype
Development officially began. What started as an AI assistant quickly evolved into a much bigger vision: one platform where AI could write, code, automate tasks, research, and connect with the tools people use every day.
Late 2025
Launching Orbit
After months of development and feedback from early users, Orbit launched publicly. New models, automation features, and developer tools were added rapidly, laying the foundation for the Orbit ecosystem.

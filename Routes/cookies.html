01 / Scope of Telemetry пакета

Cookies are modular data variables saved on your local storage layers. Within the Orbit AI environment configuration, these nodes serve as session anchors, ensuring that our execution engine dynamically recognizes your system state during ongoing container requests.

02 / Classification Profiles

Essential Core Protocols

Mandatory configurations required for account cryptographic handshakes, secure system routes, and container load-balancing. These metrics cannot be toggled offline as they protect infrastructure safety parameters.

Analytics & System Latency

Aggregated operational metrics that help our developers map network routing efficiency and script parsing performance. Completely abstracted from specific identity layers by design.

Workspace Preferences

Retains custom adjustments inside your control layout, managing details such as dark-mode intensity, UI state flags, and persistent menu maps.

03 / Regional Directive Compliance

In balance with the ePrivacy Regulation and the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), non-essential telemetry tracking tokens remain uninitialized until your explicit permission is verified. Node operators can adjust or completely clear cookie permissions instantly inside their client browser parameters or the platform's preference hub.

Third-Party Data Processors

Specific infrastructure integrations (such as instant payment processors via Stripe or identity webhook hooks via community providers) may configure distinct tracking protocols. We recommend checking their autonomous policy updates directly.

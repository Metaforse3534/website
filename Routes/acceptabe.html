Acceptable Use

STATUS: ENFORCED Last Synchronized: June 22, 2026

001 / Allowed Purpose

Orbit is intended for lawful work, learning, research, productivity, coding, writing, and automation.

002 / Illegal Use

Users may not use Orbit to break laws, evade restrictions, facilitate crime, or violate rights.

003 / Abuse Prevention

Abusive requests, spam, harassment, exploitation, impersonation, and coordinated manipulation are prohibited.

004 / Security Testing

Unauthorized scanning, stress testing, credential attacks, vulnerability exploitation, or disruption is not allowed.

005 / Harmful Content

Malware, phishing, weaponization, doxxing, sexual exploitation, or dangerous instructions are prohibited.

006 / Model Extraction

Users may not scrape, reverse engineer, clone, extract, resell, or bypass protected Orbit systems.

007 / Automation Review

Automation and agent actions must remain supervised, reviewed, and appropriate for the requested task.

008 / High Risk Use

High-risk domains require qualified human review and cannot rely solely on Orbit decisions.

009 / Account Integrity

Sharing accounts, access codes, API keys, billing access, or model locks without permission is prohibited.

010 / Rights Respect

Users must respect copyrights, privacy rights, contracts, platform rules, and data ownership.

011 / Enforcement

Violations may lead to rate limits, content removal, token revocation, suspension, or termination.

012 / Reporting

Security concerns, abuse reports, or uncertainty about allowed use should be sent to support.

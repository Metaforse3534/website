Privacy reference points

Built for school review workflows.

Orbit does not claim automatic legal compliance for every school. Instead, the schools workflow is shaped around the checks institutions commonly ask for: student record handling, children privacy rules, EU personal data processing, and human-centered AI policy.

  • FERPA is the U.S. student record privacy framework schools often evaluate for education records.
  • COPPA guidance for ed tech is relevant when services are used by children under 13 in school contexts.
  • GDPR is the EU privacy framework for personal data processing, including education deployments in Europe.
  • UNESCO guidance on generative AI in education emphasizes a human-centered approach and long-term policy planning.

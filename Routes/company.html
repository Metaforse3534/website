About Orbit AI

Built on the belief that AI should earn trust

Orbit AI is an intelligent workspace developed by Orbit Systems B.V. It brings together AI chat, browser automation, coding assistance, workflow automation, file analysis, voice workflows, and team collaboration in one coherent environment.

The company was founded on an observation: AI tools were growing more capable, but not more trustworthy. Orbit exists to close that gap — to build software that performs reliably, handles data responsibly, and earns the confidence of the people who depend on it.

Mission

What we are here to do

To build intelligent software that makes people more capable, more informed, and more effective — without asking them to trade away their privacy, security, or autonomy to get there.

Vision

Where we are building toward

A future where AI serves as a reliable extension of human intelligence — present when needed, honest about what it knows, respectful of data, and genuinely useful across the full range of work people do.

Core Principles

Five commitments behind everything we build

These are not aspirations. They are the constraints we design around.

Trust

Honest communication. No exaggerated claims. We explain what the technology can do, and what it cannot. Trust is built through consistent, verifiable behavior — not marketing.

Privacy

Your data is not for sale. Orbit complies with GDPR and treats privacy as an engineering constraint — designed in from the start, not applied as a legal afterthought.

Security

Security is fundamental, not an afterthought. Encryption in transit, access controls, secure development practices, and a public security policy with responsible disclosure.

Reliability

Orbit is built for real use. Stable, quality-first development with honest versioning. We do not oversell what exists today, and we do not ship features before they are ready.

Human-Centered AI

AI that amplifies human judgment, not AI that replaces it. Orbit is designed to work with people — expanding capability while keeping the human firmly in control of what matters.

What We Build

Privacy

Your data is not our product

Orbit does not collect user data for advertising. Your conversation content, files, and personal information exist in Orbit to make the product work for you — not to be monetized, profiled, or shared without a clear, legitimate reason.

Orbit is subject to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and operated from the Netherlands. A Data Processing Agreement is available for business customers.

Read the Privacy Policy
No advertising data use

Your usage data is never sold or shared with advertisers. Orbit is a paid product — the business model is the subscription, not the data.

GDPR compliant

Orbit complies with EU data protection law. You have the right to access your data, request deletion, and understand how it is used.

DPA for businesses

Account security

Access controls, authentication, and session management protect accounts from unauthorized access.

Encryption

Secure development

Responsible disclosure

Security

Security is fundamental, not an add-on

Orbit treats security as a core property of the platform — present in every architectural decision, not bolted on at the end of development.

This covers account security, data encryption, infrastructure monitoring, secure development practices, and the integrity of the AI systems themselves. No internet service is perfectly secure; Orbit is honest about that and works to minimize risk accordingly.

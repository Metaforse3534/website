Orbit Rules

These are the plain-language rules for using Orbit AI. They complement the Terms, Privacy Policy, and Acceptable Use Policy.

01

Use Orbit to help, build, and learn

Use Orbit for legitimate questions, coding, research, content, documents, study help, workflows, and productivity. Do not use it to harm people, break laws, deceive others, or attack systems.

02

Review AI output

Orbit can be wrong. Check important facts, calculations, sources, code, files, and generated content. Do not treat Orbit as a licensed medical, legal, financial, or safety professional.

03

Stay responsible for actions

You are responsible for actions Orbit performs at your request, including opening sites or apps, writing files, running agent workflows, creating artifacts, and drafting browser forms. Review consequential actions before confirming them.

04

Protect private information

Do not share passwords, authentication codes, payment-card details, private keys, or information you do not have permission to use. Only upload files you are allowed to process.

05

Do not abuse models, access, or credits

Do not share access codes without permission, bypass model locks, manipulate billing, scrape Orbit, overload services, reverse engineer protected components, or use multiple accounts to evade limits or enforcement.

06

Respect people and ownership

Do not use Orbit for harassment, exploitation, impersonation, non-consensual content, privacy invasion, plagiarism presented as original work, or infringement of copyrights and other rights.

07

Keep agents under supervision

Agent teams and automations can produce incorrect or unexpected results. Review goals, steps, sources, and final outputs. Do not place Orbit automations in charge of high-impact decisions or critical infrastructure.

08

Use support honestly

Provide accurate information in support tickets. Do not spam tickets, threaten staff, impersonate admins, or submit malicious files or links.

09

Enforcement

Orbit may restrict models, suspend features, remove content, revoke codes, or suspend accounts when these rules are violated. Serious illegal activity or security threats may be reported to the appropriate authorities.

Unsure whether something is allowed? Stop before running it and contact support through the signed-in Orbit app or email support@orbitdev.org.

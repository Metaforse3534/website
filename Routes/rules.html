Orbit Rules

These are the plain-language rules for using Orbit AI. They complement the Terms, Privacy Policy, and Acceptable Use Policy.

001

Use Orbit to help, build, and learn

Review AI output

Stay responsible for actions

004

Protect private information

Do not share passwords, authentication codes, payment details, private keys, or data you cannot process.

005

Do not abuse models, access, or credits

006

Respect people and ownership

007

Keep agents under supervision

Orbit AI Workspace

Orbit AI is an AI workspace for browser automation, AI agents, workflow automation, coding, research, file analysis, voice workflows, and team collaboration.

One Workspace for AI Work

Orbit AI is designed to connect the parts of modern AI work that are often split across separate products: chat, browser context, research, files, coding, agents, automation, and team workflows. Users can move from a question to a plan, from research to code, and from files to useful output inside one workspace.

Workspace Capabilities

Capability How Orbit AI Helps
Browser automation Use browser-aware assistance for page context, research, summaries, repetitive browser work, and productivity automation.
AI agents Coordinate multi-step workflows such as research, planning, coding support, file analysis, browser tasks, and team follow-up.
Coding assistance Get help with code generation, debugging, explanations, technical research, project planning, and developer workflow automation.
Research and files Analyze documents, summarize material, extract relevant details, and turn raw information into briefs, plans, code, or documentation.
Team collaboration Use one AI workspace for planning, writing, coding, automation, documentation, decisions, and project coordination.
